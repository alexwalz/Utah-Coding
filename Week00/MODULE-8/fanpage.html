The University of Utah is the best University in all of the land. The reasons for this are

  1. Great Academics
  2. Beautiful Campus
  3. New Buildings
  4. Tech Oriented
  5. GREAT FOOTBALL TEAM

I first became a Utah Football fan when I was 9 years old. My Best friend was a huge fan of the "Y", however the two of us were naturally competative, so of course I wanted to choose the opposite of him, thus my obsession came at a very young age.

Over time, my passion for the U's football team has grown stronger and stronger. I knew I had a problem when I checked my pulse after one of the close games and it read at a whopping 140 beats per minute. Yep you read that right... just from a football game. It's become a problem, certaily an addiction.

My wife and I try to travel to at least one away game every year. My bucket-list goal, before I die, is to watch the Utes play an away football game in every Pac-12 football arena. So far we have seen them in 2. one-sixth of the way there isn't too bad for my late twenties...I guess.

One of my favorite things to do before football season is to get myself pumped up by watching some highlights of the previous season. Highlights like this one!

There is nothing quite like the BYU vs UTAH rivalry and it always makes it that much better when my team comes out on the winning side.

If you want to follow Utah Football like I do, you may want to check out Their Website, as this is where the update all the most recent and relevant news.

Have fun and GO UTES!!!
